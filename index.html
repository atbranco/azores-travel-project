Why the Azores

Azores, the paradise in the middle of the Atlantic Ocean

"In the realm of legend, some associate the Azores to the Atlantis, the mythical island kingdom quoted by Plato.As for history,it was with the Portuguese Maritime Discoveries, that the Azores were definitely registered in the map of Europe in 1427 or 1431."

"The Azores are a destination for nature and experiences, focused on the preservation of resources and the preservation of the archipelago's natural legacy. This path, earned it the distinction of the first archipelago in the world to win the seal "Sustainable Tourism Destination", under the EarthCheck Sustainable Destination programme." (source:visitazores.com)
